# ARCHITECTURE: Property_Portal_Trust_Shield

## MOAT
- **Market Angle**: Property fraud is rampant on portals (fake listings, expired EPCs). 'Trust Shield' offers a local validation layer.
- **Operator Advantage**: Automates cross-referencing with public government registers (e.g., EPC, Title Deeds).
- **Adoption Ease**: Integrates into existing property management workflows.
- **Traction Gravity**: Trust is the ultimate portal KPI; automated validation becomes a mandatory 'Verified' badge.

## BEST
- **Benefits**: Eliminates manual compliance checks for listings.
- **Evidence**: Integration with simulated gov.uk EPC registers.
- **Speed**: Checks property compliance status in real-time.
- **Trade-off**: Requires access to external (simulated) data registers.

## 10X GAIN-TO-PAIN RATIO
- **Pain**: Manual title and certificate verification takes 30 mins per listing.
- **Gain**: Automated check in 10 seconds.
- **Ratio**: >100X improvement in verification speed.
