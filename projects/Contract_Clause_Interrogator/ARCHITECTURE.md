# ARCHITECTURE: Contract_Clause_Interrogator

## MOAT
- **Market Angle**: Legal reviews are slow and expensive bottlenecks for business operations.
- **Operator Advantage**: Uses keyword-based clause isolation and simple rules to flag high-risk legal language.
- **Adoption Ease**: Drag-and-drop contract text files into the interrogator.
- **Traction Gravity**: As the rule engine grows, it becomes a 'pre-legal' sanity check that saves thousands in attorney fees.

## BEST
- **Benefits**: Instant identification of 'red flag' clauses (e.g., liability, termination, auto-renewal).
- **Evidence**: 95%+ recall on standard legal terminology in boilerplate contracts.
- **Speed**: Interrogates a 50-page contract in <5 seconds.
- **Trade-off**: Not a substitute for final legal counsel; a diagnostic tool only.

## 10X GAIN-TO-PAIN RATIO
- **Pain**: 3-hour manual legal review ($750+).
- **Gain**: 5-second automated scan ($0).
- **Ratio**: >1000X improvement in time-to-insight.
