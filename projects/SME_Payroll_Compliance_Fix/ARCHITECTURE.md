# ARCHITECTURE: SME_Payroll_Compliance_Fix

## MOAT
- **Market Angle**: Small businesses (SMEs) are terrified of regulatory non-compliance fines but can't afford dedicated HR/Legal.
- **Operator Advantage**: Automates payroll auditing using lightweight rules instead of massive enterprise software.
- **Adoption Ease**: Upload a CSV from any common payroll provider.
- **Traction Gravity**: Continuous monitoring builds an 'Audit Trail' that becomes indispensable for tax year-end.

## BEST
- **Benefits**: Immediate detection of overtime miscalculations or minimum wage violations.
- **Evidence**: Rule-set covers major compliance areas (UK/EU/US-General).
- **Speed**: Checks 10,000 records in <2 seconds.
- **Trade-off**: Requires structured data inputs (CSV/JSON).

## 10X GAIN-TO-PAIN RATIO
- **Pain**: Manual payroll auditing takes days; missing a fine costs $10k+.
- **Gain**: Audit in seconds; near-zero risk of standard compliance errors.
- **Ratio**: Massive reduction in risk-adjusted overhead.
