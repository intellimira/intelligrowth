# Sovereign Intelligence Layer: SaaS Pain-First Blueprint

This document defines the operational mechanics for a "Shadow Operator" discovery agent, transitioning from passive scraping to proactive sovereign intelligence.

## 1. High-Precision Sourcing (The Ingestion Stack)
*   **B2B Feature Gaps (G2 & Capterra):** Scan 1-star/2-star reviews for "doesn't have", "too complex", "missing".
*   **Workaround Job Boards (Upwork):** Identify repetitive manual tasks (e.g., PDF to Spreadsheet). *Logic: $20/hr manual task = $50/mo automation opportunity.*
*   **Frustration Peaks (Reddit):** Target r/microsaas, r/SaaS, r/startups on **Mondays/Tuesdays**.
*   **Search Intent (Regex):** Long-tail (10+ words) troubleshooting queries.

## 2. Autonomous Discovery Mechanics
*   **The "I Wish" Operator:** `site:reddit.com "[niche]" "I wish"` or `"overkill"`.
*   **Trigger Event Detection:** Pricing shocks (incumbent hikes) or Policy changes (GDPR/Compliance panics).
*   **Linguistic Signals:** Prioritize long-form technical rants (229+ characters) as feature specs.

## 3. The Meat Grinder (Qualification)
*   **The "Receipt" Filter:** Evidence of historical spending ("I spent $400/mo and it still breaks").
*   **The "Ugly Glue" Signal:** 4+ Zapier zaps or complex spreadsheets.
*   **Budget Owner Profile:** Authority to swipe credit cards (Operations/Finance Managers).
*   **Magic Number Validation:** 1,000+ monthly searches, <5 direct competitors, 3+ Reddit threads with 50+ comments.

## 4. Agentic Orchestration & Problem CRM
*   **Automated Trigger:** 24/7 data fetching and theme clustering.
*   **Contextual Analysis:** Help-first, sell-second scoring (0-100).
*   **Autonomous Outreach:** "Mom Test" approved questions focused on past behavior.
*   **CRM Enrichment:** Tracking tech stack, frustration level, and "Underlying Job".
*   **The Truth Serum (Workflow Audit):** Self-service "Interactive Workflow Diagnostic". *No data provided = Vitamin; Detailed workflow data provided = Painkiller.*

## 5. Delivery Blueprint (Quick Wins)
*   **Platform Arbitrage:** Porting successful features (e.g., Airtable forms -> Notion).
*   **MCP Search:** IndieStack MCP registry search to prevent "Token Reinvention".
*   **Manual Concierge MVP:** Manual outcome delivery for first 5-10 customers before final code.
