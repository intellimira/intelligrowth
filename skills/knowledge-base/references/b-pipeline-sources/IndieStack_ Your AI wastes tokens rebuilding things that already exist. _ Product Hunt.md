Hey PH! We're Pat and Ed — two uni students in Cardiff (zoology and teaching, not CS). Our issue: AI keeps rebuilding auth, billing, analytics, even flashcard apps from scratch — things indie creators already solved. Meanwhile, those indie creations sit on GitHub with 12 stars, invisible to the AI agents that could be recommending them. IndieStack fixes both sides: 793 indie creations (and growing!) with an MCP server. Your AI searches what exists before building from scratch. And if you've built something useful — a CLI tool, a library, a game, a SaaS — list it so everyone's AI can find it instead of reinventing it.
