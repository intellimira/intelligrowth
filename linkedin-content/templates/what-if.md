# What-If Template

**Pattern:** Speculation → Possibility → Invitation

---

## Hook
[Provocative "What if..." opening]

## Possibility
[What could this enable? What would change?]

## Implication
[Why does this matter? What shifts?]

## Invitation
[Question to engage readers]

---

## Example Structure

> What if every business had an AI employee that never slept?
> 
> Not a chatbot. Not an assistant.
> A dedicated agent that knows your business inside out.
> 
> It knows your clients, your processes, your blind spots.
> It learns from every interaction.
> It surfaces opportunities before you see them.
> 
> That's not sci-fi. That's what I'm building.
> 
> What's one task you'd delegate to an AI that knew your business?
