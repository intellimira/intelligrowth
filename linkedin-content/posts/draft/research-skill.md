# Most people use AI to search. I built one that researches.

Most people use AI like a search engine:
- Prompt → answer → context disappears → repeat

Every new conversation means re-explaining:
- Your role and expertise
- Your preferred format
- Your quality standards
- Your constraints

I got tired of that cycle.

So I built a skill that researches autonomously.

**How it works:**
1. I give it a topic or URL
2. It analyzes the content through two passes:
   - First: Persona Council (multiple expert perspectives)
   - Second: Specialization Layer (deep technical detail)
3. It outputs a curated SKILL.md - a reusable knowledge module

**The result:**
- Research that used to take hours now takes minutes
- Knowledge compounds instead of disappearing
- I can query my "memory mesh" for past learnings

The real AI advantage isn't better prompts.

It's building systems where intelligence persists.
