# I monetised my AI experiments. Here's the system.

Most people use AI to:
- Write emails
- Generate images
- Summarise documents

Useful. But not revenue-generating.

I built a system that turns every experiment into potential income.

**The Shadow Ops Framework:**
1. Scout → Identify a business pain point
2. Research → AI-assisted deep dive
3. Ground → Build a prototype
4. Pitch → Value proposition
5. Build → Delivery
6. Monetise → Revenue track
7. Close → Repeat

**What I've learned:**
- Problems worth solving have patterns
- AI accelerates research, not judgment
- The money is in systems, not tools

Every project I run becomes:
- A case study (content)
- A skill (reusable)
- A potential service (revenue)
