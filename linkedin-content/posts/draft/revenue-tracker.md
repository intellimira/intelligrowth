# I stopped guessing about my income. Here's how.

Every freelancer knows this feeling:
- "Did that client pay?"
- "What's my actual profit on this project?"
- "Why does my bank account never match my invoices?"

I built an AI system that tracks every deal from prospect to paid.

**What it tracks:**
- Revenue per project
- Development costs (my time)
- AI costs (what I spend on Claude/Gemini)
- Infrastructure costs
- True profit margin

**The result:**
I know exactly which projects are worth my time.
I know my real ROI per client.
I know when to fire a client.

Most people run their business on vibes.
I run mine on data.
