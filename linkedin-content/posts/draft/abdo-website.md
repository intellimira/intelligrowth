# I built a client website in hours. Here's what most agencies miss.

A client needed a website. Traditional quote: £3,000+, 4-week timeline.

I built it in an afternoon.

Not because I'm special. Because I used AI the way it was meant to be used - as a force multiplier, not a replacement.

**What I did:**
1. Extracted brand colors from their existing materials (jpeg → hex palette)
2. Used AI to generate the site structure based on their industry
3. Built the components with pre-tested templates
4. Added their specific products and content

**The result:**
- A dark-themed, mobile-responsive site
- Product grid layout with their actual inventory
- Wholesale inquiry integration via QR code
- All based on their brand, not a generic template

**What most agencies miss:**
The real value isn't the code. It's understanding what the client actually needs and translating that into the right technical decisions.

Tools are commoditized. Judgment is rare.
