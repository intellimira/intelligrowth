# I run my own AI infrastructure. Here's why it matters.

Most people pay monthly for AI tools.
- ChatGPT: $20/month
- Claude: $20/month  
- Perplexity: $20/month
- Image generation: $10/month

Total: $70+/month. Forever.

I run local AI instead:
- Ollama for LLMs (offline, unlimited)
- Local embeddings for memory
- Self-hosted agents

**The costs:**
- One-time server cost: ~£200
- Electricity: negligible
- Total after 1 year: less than 3 months of subscriptions

**The benefits:**
- Complete privacy (no data leaves my machine)
- No rate limits
- Custom models fine-tuned on my data
- Works without internet

**The reality check:**
Cloud AI is great for experimenting.
Local AI is great for production.

I'm building toward the latter.
